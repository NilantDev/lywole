If the word is in title and in a lyrics then you get 1 point for every unique letter (UL).

If the word is not in title but in a lyrics then the points are calculated as progression. For the first UL you get 1 point, for the second - 2 point, for the third - 3 point, etc. Then all points are summarized. For example, for the word 'yesterday', you'll get 28 points.

YESTERDAY - 7 ULs
Y 1
E 2
S 3
T 4
R 5
D 6
A 7
SUM: 28

Also to get points word should contain open letters (The letters that wasn't in previous guessed words).
